Our Story

For over 140 years, Hotel Management has been the hospitality industry’s magazine of record. We are dedicated to providing the latest news, insights, and trends for the hospitality community. Whether you’re a hotel owner, developer, operator, broker, or financier, our editorial staff curates content that matters to you.

What We Cover

Hotel Investment: Stay informed about investment opportunities and strategies in the ever-evolving hospitality landscape.
Development: Explore the latest developments in hotel design, architecture, and construction.
Operations: Learn best practices for efficient hotel operations, guest services, and staff management.
Technology: Discover cutting-edge solutions and innovations that enhance guest experiences and streamline processes.
Brand Strategies: Dive into brand positioning, marketing, and differentiation strategies.
Sharing Economy: Understand the impact of platforms like Airbnb and how they shape the industry.
